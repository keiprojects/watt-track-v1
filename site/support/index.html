Watt Track Support

App: Watt Track

Current version: 1.0.0

Support email: support@keiprojects.com

Getting Help

Email support with your app version, device model, operating system version, and a short description of what you were doing when the issue happened.

Common Fixes

  • If weather is unavailable, check your internet connection or update the saved location in Settings.
  • If reminders do not appear, confirm notification permission is enabled for Watt Track in system settings.
  • If an imported backup fails, confirm the selected file is a Watt Track JSON backup.
  • If values look wrong after changing reading modes, review earlier cumulative readings in History.

Data and Privacy

Watt Track stores profile, readings, settings, costs, and local backup restore points on your device. Review the privacy policy for details.
